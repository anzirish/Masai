The Myth Behind the Man

Kvothe, the gifted arcanist and infamous adventurer, is more than the sum of the tales whispered in taverns. From his days with the Edema Ruh to mastering Sympathy at the University, his journey is one of legend, loss, and learning. But is the truth more powerful than the myth?

The Archives and the Name of the Wind

Deep within the University lies the Archives — a place of boundless knowledge and buried secrets. Kvothe’s obsession with naming and uncovering the lost Name of the Wind brings him closer to an ancient power that defies comprehension, and deeper into the mystery surrounding the Chandrian.
