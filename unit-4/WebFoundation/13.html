Welcome to Galadriel's Realm

I am Galadriel, the Lady of Lothlórien, an elf of the First Age and bearer of one of the Three Elven Rings of Power. I have witnessed the rise and fall of kingdoms and have walked among the stars and shadows of Middle-earth. Wisdom, light, and resilience are the foundations of my being.

In the golden woods of Lothlórien, I find joy in stargazing, singing the songs of the Eldar, nurturing the mallorn trees, and studying ancient elven lore. I also cherish the art of telepathy and exploring the mysteries of time and vision through the Mirror of Galadriel.
